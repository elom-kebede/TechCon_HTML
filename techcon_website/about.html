Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers passionate about open-source technology. Over the years, it has evolved into a global platform where technology leaders share insights, startups showcase innovation, and attendees explore future-forward ideas.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to ignite innovation, inspire collaboration, and shape the future of technology by connecting creators, thinkers, and builders from around the world.
